About Me

Alyssa Alecci is a scholar of visual design who spends her time coding, drawing, and daydreaming about drawing and coding. With a passion for creating, brainstorming, and giving back to her communities, she strives to create polished and intuitive work and a great working environment for those around her.

She loves teaching, storytelling, and editing, and has aided a number of her creative peers in their endeavors. In her spare time, she writes and draws a drama/horror webcomic called Lapse, and does volunteer administrative duties and web design for the SpiderForest Webcomic Collective.

She also really loves dogs and black tea.
